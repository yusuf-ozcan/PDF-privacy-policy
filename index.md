# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 28, 2026  
**Developer:** Yusuf Özcan  
**Application:** PDF Converter - Smart Scanner (the "Service")

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Application handles information when you use the Service.

The Application is designed with a **privacy-first, offline-first architecture** and does not collect or monetize user data in any form.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We do **not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or user-generated content**.

This includes:
- Personal information (such as name, email, phone number)
- Scanned documents, images, or generated PDFs
- OCR-processed content
- Device contacts, location data, or media library content
- Usage behavior tied to identity

### On-Device Processing

All core features, including:
- Document scanning
- Image-to-PDF conversion
- OCR (text recognition)
- File processing and editing

are performed entirely **on your device** using native iOS frameworks.

Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any external server.

---

## 2. Apple Ecosystem & Technical Data

The Application is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may process certain **anonymous and aggregated technical data**, depending on your iOS settings.

This may include:
- Crash reports
- Device model
- iOS version
- App performance metrics (e.g., launch time)
- Region and language settings

This data:
- Is anonymized by Apple before being shared
- Cannot be used by us to identify you
- Is used solely to improve stability, fix bugs, and enhance performance

We do not receive personally identifiable information from Apple.

---

## 3. Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

### Camera
Used only to scan physical documents directly within the app.

### Photo Library / Files
Used only to select existing images or documents for PDF conversion.

All permission-based data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

You can manage or revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### No Advertising
The Application contains:
- No advertisements
- No ad networks
- No tracking for advertising purposes
- No use of advertising identifiers (IDFA)

### No Tracking SDKs
We do not use any third-party analytics, marketing, or user-tracking SDKs.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security

We do not operate any backend servers or cloud storage systems.

All files created or imported in the Application:
- Are stored locally on your device
- Remain under your control
- Can be deleted at any time by removing the app or deleting files manually

Security is provided by the iOS operating system. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Since no data is collected or transmitted, the Application is safe for general use. If you believe any issue exists regarding a child’s usage, please contact us.

---

## 7. International Compliance (GDPR / CCPA / KVKK)

The Application is designed with **privacy-by-design principles** and inherently aligns with major data protection regulations, including:

- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- KVKK (Law on the Protection of Personal Data – Turkey)

Since no personal data is collected or processed, user rights such as access, deletion, or portability are handled locally by the user (e.g., deleting files or uninstalling the Application).

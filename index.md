# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 28, 2026  
**Developer:** Yusuf Özcan  
**Application:** PDF Converter - Smart Scanner (the "Service")

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Application handles information when you use the Service.

The Application is designed with a **privacy-first, offline-first architecture** and does not collect, store, transmit, or monetize any user data.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or user-generated content.

This includes:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Scanned documents, images, or generated PDFs
- OCR-processed content
- Device contacts, location data, or media library content
- Usage data or analytics tied to a user identity
- Device identifiers used for tracking purposes

All processing is performed locally on your device.

---

## 2. On-Device Processing

All core features are executed entirely on-device using native iOS frameworks, including:

- Document scanning
- Image-to-PDF conversion
- OCR (text recognition)
- File processing and editing

Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any external server or cloud service.

---

## 3. Apple Ecosystem & Technical Data

The Application is distributed via the Apple App Store.

Apple may collect and process certain anonymous and aggregated technical data depending on your iOS settings, including:

- Crash reports
- Device model
- iOS version
- App performance metrics
- Region and language settings

This data:
- Is anonymized by Apple before being shared with developers
- Cannot be used to identify you
- Is used only to improve app stability and performance

We do not receive any personally identifiable information or device-level identifiers.

---

## 4. Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

### Camera
Used only to scan physical documents.

### Photo Library / Files
Used only to import images or documents for PDF conversion.

All permission-based data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

You can manage or revoke permissions at any time via iOS Settings.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

### No Advertising
The Application contains:
- No advertisements
- No ad networks
- No tracking for advertising purposes
- No use of advertising identifiers (IDFA)

### No Tracking SDKs
We do not use any third-party analytics, marketing, or tracking SDKs.

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

We do not operate any backend servers or cloud storage systems.

All files created or imported in the Application:
- Are stored locally on your device
- Remain fully under your control
- Can be deleted at any time by removing the application or deleting files manually

Security is ensured by the iOS operating system’s built-in protections, including sandboxing and encryption.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect any personal information from any user, including children.

Since no data is collected or transmitted, the Application is safe for general use.

---

## 8. International Compliance (GDPR / CCPA / KVKK)

The Application is designed with privacy-by-design principles and aligns with major data protection regulations, including:

- GDPR (European Union)
- CCPA (California)
- KVKK (Turkey)

Since no personal data is collected or processed, all privacy rights (access, deletion, portability) are handled locally by the user (e.g., deleting files or uninstalling the app).

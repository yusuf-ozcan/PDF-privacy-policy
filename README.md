# PDF Converter - Smart Scanner

A fast, offline-first, privacy-focused PDF scanner and converter for iOS.

## Overview

PDF Converter - Smart Scanner is a lightweight mobile application that allows users to scan documents, convert images to PDF, and perform OCR (text recognition) directly on their device.

The app is designed with a **privacy-first philosophy**, meaning all processing happens locally on the device with no data collection or external servers.

---

## Key Features

- 📄 Scan documents using your device camera
- 🖼️ Convert images to high-quality PDFs
- ✍️ OCR (text recognition) on-device
- 📁 Import images or files from your device
- ⚡ Fast, lightweight, and optimized performance
- 🔒 100% offline processing
- 🚫 No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking

---

## Privacy First Design

This application is built with strict privacy principles:

- No user data is collected
- No documents or images are uploaded
- No analytics or tracking SDKs are used
- No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- No accounts or sign-in required

All processing is performed **locally on your device using native iOS frameworks**.

---

## Security

Your files remain under your control at all times.  
Security is handled by iOS system-level protections, including sandboxing and encryption.

We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode.

---

- iOS (iPhone & iPad)
